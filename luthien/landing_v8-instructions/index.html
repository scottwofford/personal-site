Luthien QA Trial

Walk through our product as a new user. Think aloud the whole time — we want the good, the bad, and the ugly.

Do this async, or book a call to walk through it together.

Part 1: Start Recording & Warm Up ~5 min

Start a screen recording (Loom, Zoom, QuickTime, OBS) with webcam + audio, and an AI note-taker (SuperWhisper, Otter.ai) for a transcript.

Warm up: give us a 30-second tour of your dock or taskbar — what's open, what are your pinned apps? No wrong answers, just get your brain-to-mouth pipeline going.

Part 2: Your Dev Workflow ~10 min

Share your screen and walk us through:

  1. Typical day: What does a normal coding or ops day look like?
  2. AI in your flow: Where do AI tools (Cursor, Claude Code, Copilot, etc.) show up? What makes you reach for them?
  3. Code review: How do you handle reviews — who signs off?
  4. Frustrations: Last time an AI assistant was really frustrating — what happened?
  5. Worst case: What keeps you up at night? Security, spending, privacy?
  6. Workarounds: How do you cope today? If you had a magic wand to prevent one AI failure, what would it do?

Part 4: README Quick Start ~45–60 min

README.md (PR #179 branch)

Read the intro sections first — note what clicks and what doesn't. Then follow the Quick Start step by step.

Follow every step exactly as written. Don't skip ahead or improvise. If stuck on a step for >10 min, note where you got stuck, skip it, and keep going. Docker's first build can be slow (~5–10 min) — that's expected.

For each step: did it work? If not, exact error + screenshot. How long did it take?
